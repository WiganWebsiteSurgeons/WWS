About us

Our story

From local roots to
global standards.

Wigan Website Surgeons was founded with a simple belief: businesses in the North West deserve the same quality of digital work as those in London, New York or anywhere else.

We started as a small studio in Wigan and have grown into a trusted partner for businesses across Greater Manchester and beyond. The "Surgeons" in our name isn't just branding — it's a philosophy. We approach every project with precision, care and expertise.
